Rice

Sesame sprinkled, white rice in a bowl

Simple as it gets, this recipe should get you up and running for whatever hurdle that may present.

With a wide array of suitable side dishes, preparing a full meal will be easy regardless of taste.

Ingredients

  • Rice 150g
  • Water 100ml

Steps

  1. Wash rice
  2. Put rice and water in a pan
  3. Cook in low heat, lid closed
  4. As water starts boiling, leave the lid open just a bit
  5. Cook it until you feel it's done
