Pan-Seared Salmon

Live salmon being held by a fisherman

While it may sound complicated for many, pan-searing a salmon can be done in no time.

The only think you should pay attention to is when to flip the salmon so it doesn't get over nor undercooked.

Ingredients

  • Salmon 2 fillets
  • Olive oil 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Put the olive oil in a pan and heat it up
  2. Lay the salmon fillets on the pan
  3. As you notice the fillet gets cooked just about halfway its height, flip it
  4. Pay close attention to the fillets as they cook. Right after the heat gets its other half cooked, turn off the heat and serve it
